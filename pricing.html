Bronze Service: $5,000

This service can be purchased for jobs that require a total of 40 hours or less of work with a two worker team.

Machinery costs included:

  • Forklift
  • Scissor Lift

Other necessary machinery will cost you extra.

Silver Service: $10,000

This service can be purchased for jobs that require a total of 40 hours or less of work with a four worker team.

Machinery costs included:

  • Forklift
  • Scissor Lift
  • Crane
  • Bulldozer

Other necessary machinery will cost you extra.

Gold Service: $20,000

This service can be purchased for jobs that require a total of 80 hours or less of work with a four worker team.

Machinery costs included:

  • Forklift
  • Scissor Lift
  • Crane
  • Bulldozer
  • Dump Truck
  • Cement Truck
  • Excavator

Other necessary machinery will cost you extra.
