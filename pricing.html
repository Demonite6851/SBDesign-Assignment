Bronze Service

Price: $5,000.
This service can be purchased for jobs that require a total of 40 hours or less of work, with two workers working on it.

Machinery costs included:

  • Forklift
  • Scissor Lift

Other necessary machinery will cost you extra.

Silver Service

Price: $10,000.
This service can be purchased for jobs that require a total of 40 hours or less of work, with four workers working on it.

Machinery costs included:

  • Forklift
  • Scissor Lift
  • Crane
  • Bulldozer

Other necessary machinery will cost you extra.

Gold Service

Price: $20,000.

Machinery costs included:

  • Forklift
  • Scissor Lift
  • Crane
